Sweet and CornSoup
.

Crushed and whole sweet corn comes together with a colourful assortment of juicy vegetables in this sumptuous soup.

Corn soup is a soup made of the typical sweetcorn. It is an excellent soup base, before you start a meal. Corn, a staple crop for many Native American tribes has led to corn soup being a primary food among them. The soup is high in nutrients, Vitamin B1, Vitamin B5, and Vitamin C. Corn also helps in lowering cholesterol levels in the body by reducing the levels of blood sugar in diabetics too.
