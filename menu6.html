Prawns Fry

A simple delicious and flavorful recipe is shallow fried in little bit of oil.

The term "prawn" is used particularly in the United Kingdom, Ireland, and Commonwealth nations. Prawns are a great source of high quality protein, and provide some of the most important vitamins and minerals that make up a healthy diet. They are surprisingly low in calories and are made up of extremely healthy cholesterol. In fact, according to the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition, eating prawns is part of a heart healthy diet.
