Paneer-Tikka-Masala

This healthy vegetarian dinner is a creamy, saucy curry with paneer (if you’ve never had paneer, you’ll love it), fresh vegetables, and warm spices.

You need a vacation,somewhere warm and far away ? Pull out your spices, turn on some music, and let’s take a trip to India with Paneer Tikka Masala. Indian food is deeply and warmly spiced, but it’s not necessarily “spicy” in the sense of “hot.” It’s a delightful, enriching cuisine to explore, and this easy Paneer Tikka Masala is the ideal place to start.Paneer is a firm, mild, and pleasant cheese, similar to a curd you’d find in cottage cheese. except (and this is important) it tastes way, way better than that description sounds.
